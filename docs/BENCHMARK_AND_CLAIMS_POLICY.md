# Benchmark And Claims Policy

## Allowed Wording

- "Under a stricter past-to-future benchmark, ALTHEA showed materially stronger prioritization than simple baseline queues."
- "Protocol B benchmark Recall@Top 10% reached 0.5522, outperforming chronological, amount, and weighted heuristic baselines."
- "These results are internal benchmark evidence and not live-bank proof."

## Not Allowed

- "Validated on live bank data"
- "Proven to reduce bank workload by 43% in production"
- "Detects SARs automatically"
- "Replaces AML monitoring"
- "Decides SAR/STR filing"

## Required Caveats

Live pilots have not been completed unless a signed bank pilot report says otherwise. ROI numbers are illustrative or modeled until measured in a controlled pilot. ALTHEA provides investigation support only; final SAR/STR filing decisions must be made by authorized compliance staff.
